We Love Opera!

Our mission is to create greater interest in opera and musical theater in the Tampa Bay area.

We offer introduction to opera lectures at various venues in the Tampa Bay area and monthly social meetings that include lunch and operatic entertainment.

You are welcome to browse our site, join us for any of our Events, or contact us for more information.

We Love Opera!

Our mission is to create greater interest in opera and musical theater in the Tampa Bay area.

We offer introduction to opera lectures at various venues in the Tampa Bay area and we present monthly showings of videos of live opera performances in partnership with Safety Harbor Library (see events). In addition we have monthly social meetings that include lunch and operatic entertainment

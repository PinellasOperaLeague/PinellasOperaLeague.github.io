Luncheon meetings are a fun way to socialize and meet others who enjoy opera and music. On the first Wednesday of the month October to June the League sponsors a luncheon at the Dunedin Country Club. 1050 Palm Blvd., Dunedin, FL 34698 (727) 733-2134. Cost is $20. Please pay at the door with exact cash or a check made out to the Pinellas Opera League.

The luncheon meetings begin at 11:00 am with a time for socializing. Business 11:30, program 11:45 and lunch 12:30.

The June 4th Luncheon is our last luncheon for this season. We begin again on October 1.

For reservations, call Nancy at 727-738-4007 by Friday, May 30. Any cancellations must be received by Saturday, May 31.

The League is obligated to pay for all reserved luncheons. No shows and reservations not cancelled by the above deadline will be billed.

The October Luncheon features Cantor Colman Reaboi. Cantor Reaboi began his singing career at age thirteen in Southfield, Michigan in the role of a Bar Mitzvah boy! He later attended the Boston Conservatory of Music where he majored in Vocal Performance and Opera. Cantor Reaboi later studied Cantorial Arts, Jewish Education and received a Bachelorâ€™s degree in Jewish Studies from Hebrew College in Netwon, MA. He was also enrolled in the Cantital Internship program of the Cantor's Assembly, and received recognition by the Cantorial Arts as a Hazzan with full membership.

In addition to his Cantorial work, Cantor Reaboi was a member and a soloist of the distinguished Zamir Chorale. He was also a guest Cantor for the Prague Jewish community, where he conducted services, performed concerts and lectures at the Prague Jewish Museum. He has sung on stage in operas, Broadway musicals, and the concert stage as well as having written many plays for children and a Purim Musical called 'The Megillah According to ABBA'.

Luncheon meetings are a fun way to socialize and meet others who enjoy opera and music. On the first Wednesday of each month, October to June, the League sponsors a luncheon at the Dunedin Country Club. 1050 Palm Blvd., Dunedin, FL 34698 (727) 733-2134.

Beginning October 1, the cost for members is $22 and for non-members $25. You can pay at the door with exact cash or a check made out to the Pinellas Opera League.

The luncheon meetings begin at 11:00 am with a time for socializing. Business 11:30, program 11:45 and lunch 12:30.

Reservations must be made by Friday evening September 28, and cancellations by Saturday morning September 29th.

The League is obligated to pay for all reserved luncheons. No shows and reservations not cancelled by the above deadline will be billed.

Join us to socialize and meet other opera lovers for a fun afternoon to experience the voices of local opera talent.

Program

St. Petersburg Opera and Maestro Mark Sforzini will present members of the cast of Turandot. Julia Ebner, the cover for the slave girl Liu, and Jesse Stock, the cover for the Lord Chancellor Ping, will sing excerpts from Puccini’s masterpiece and some other surprises. Julia has sung with Florida Grand Opera, Opera North, Green Mountain Opera Festival, Virginia Opera, The Santa Fe Opera, Syracuse Opera, The Tri-Cities Opera and others. Jesse was in SPO’s production of Pagliacci and has sung in the young artist programs at Knoxville Opera and Des Moines Metro Opera and recently appeared at Opera Fayetteville.

Easter Parade Luncheon

Our Easter parade was a huge success thanks to all those who wore their Easter bonnets with all the frills upon it. There were beautiful arrays of colors worn by all the participants. The soloist for the day, Alessandra Feliciano sang the Irving Berlin song “Easter Parade” as the members paraded around the room. The prize for the prettiest hat went to Patti Baldwin, who was in a yellow ensemble. The most creative prize went to Kathleen Masalski with her parasol.

Join us!

Luncheon meetings are a fun way to socialize and meet others who enjoy opera and music. On the first Wednesday of each month, October to June, the League sponsors a luncheon at the Dunedin Country Club. 1050 Palm Blvd., Dunedin, FL 34698 (727) 733-2134.

The cost for members is $22 and for non-members $25. You can pay at the door with exact cash or a check made out to the Pinellas Opera League.

The luncheon meetings begin at 11:00 am with a time for socializing. Business 11:30, program 11:45 and lunch 12:30.

Reservations must be made by Friday evening May 1, and cancellations by Saturday morning May 2. Contact Nancy @727-738-4007. Please be sure to call in your reservations on time.

The League is obligated to pay for all reserved luncheons. No shows and reservations not cancelled by the above deadline will be billed.

Luncheon Sponsors

If you sponsor our luncheon, you get to sit at the performers’ table and chat with the artists. Please contact Nancy Jewitt at 727-738-4007. Sponsorship money is used to help pay for the performers’ meals. Thanks to Margarita and Peter Garcia who have donated $220.00 to cover the artists’ luncheons for the rest of the year.

Afternoon Tea at Moffat's Cup of Tea in Dunedin, FL

A Fundraiser for the Pinellas Opera League

On Sunday, January 11, from 1-3 p.m., we will be having an Afternoon Tea Fundraiser at Moffat's Cup of Tea on 355 Scotland St. in Dunedin. The Pinellas Opera League will be having an inside room to ourselves, but the outside space will be open to the public. Tickets are $25.00. This includes cream tea, coffee and delicious sweet and savory culinary offerings. The Pinellas Opera League will need a $50.00 deposit to hold the room. Please continue to sign up for our tea at the November luncheon, or for reservations please call Wayne Green, our Special Events Chairman, at 727-470-9537

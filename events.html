Pinellas Opera League Events

Besides our Luncheons, we organize several events you can attend for reduced prices through the Pinellas Opera League. We would love to have you there with us!

Pinellas Opera League presents DVD Opera

Saturday, February 21, 2015. Come join us to watch a DVD of “Maria Stuarda” by Gaetano Donizetti, starring Joyce DiDonato from the Metropolitan Opera.

Running time 2:23.

Safety Harbor Public Library, 101 2nd Street North, Safety Harbor, FL 34695. The library door opens at 9:30 a.m. and the video begins at 10:00 a.m.

Free and open to the public. There is free parking in back of the library. This is the third Saturday of the month. For more information call Richard Davidson at 727-242-6486

Spring Fundraiser Cruise

The POL will again have a fundraiser luncheon cruise on the “Starlight Majesty” Saturday, March 7, 2015, departing at 12:00 p.m. The cruise lasts 1 1/2 to 2 hours in the protected waters of Clearwater Harbor, so expect a very calm and relaxing trip. Tickets are $40.00 each and include a sit-down luncheon in an enclosed, climate controlled dining room featuring your choice of six entrees served with soup or salad and dessert. To order tickets download the order form or you may sign up at our luncheon. If you have further questions please contact Wayne Green, our Special Events Coordinator, at 727-470-9537.

Florida Opera Festival 2015 presented by Opera Tampa

Tickets will now be sold individually through Wayne Green until the Opera Festival begins. Be sure to attend the Florida Opera Festival. Remaining tickets are $70.00 which includes Level B seating, service fees, and free roundtrip bus transportation on a luxury motor coach. Submit your order form with check to Wayne Green or mail to Wayne at 1340 Bayshore Blvd. #407, Dunedin, FL 34698. Make checks payable to the Pinellas Opera League.

  • Rigoletto on Sunday, February 15th at 2:00 pm
  • Marcello Giordani the Met’s favorite tenor Saturday, February 28th at 7:00 pm
  • Madama Butterfly on Sunday, March 15th at 2:00 pm

Please advise if you need special assistance for seating on arrival at the Straz Center. Pickup 12:30 p.m. Clearwater Mall-Us 19 and Gulf-To-Bay (Lowe’s Garden Center parking lot) and 5:30 p.m. for the evening performance by Marcello Giordani.

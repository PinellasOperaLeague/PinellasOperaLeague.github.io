Afternoon Tea at Moffat's Cup of Tea in Dunedin, FL

A Fundraiser for the Pinellas Opera League

On Sunday, January 11, from 1-3 p.m., we will be having an Afternoon Tea Fundraiser at Moffat's Cup of Tea on 355 Scotland St. in Dunedin. The Pinellas Opera League will be having an inside room to ourselves, but the outside space will be open to the public. Tickets are $25.00. This includes cream tea, coffee and delicious sweet and savory culinary offerings. The Pinellas Opera League will need a $50.00 deposit to hold the room. Please continue to sign up for our tea at the November luncheon, or for reservations please call Wayne Green, our Special Events Chairman, at 727-470-9537

NOTE: The location given in the November newsletter for this event is incorrect. The event will be held at Moffat's Cup of Tea in Dunedin.

Florida Opera Festival 2015

A discounted festival 4-pack is again being offered to Pinellas Opera League Members for the upcoming 2015 season. Included are:

  • New Voices Concert on Sunday January 18th at 2:00 pm
  • Rigoletto on Sunday, February 15th at 2:00 pm
  • Marcello Giordani the Met’s favorite tenor Saturday, February 28th at 7:00 pm
  • Madama Butterfly on Sunday, March 15th at 2:00 pm

The festival package also includes round trip bus transportation from the Clearwater Mall (Gulf to Bay Blvd at Hwy 19) to the Straz Center for each performance. The full package cost is $195 or single performance tickets are available for $70 each. Our deadline for ticket purchases is Wednesday, December 3rd, 2014. Tickets may be purchased at our luncheons or by printing and mailing the attached order form to Wayne Green (Special Events Coordinator) at the address indicated on the form.

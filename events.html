Evenings with the Maestro: Opera in the Movies

Maestro Sforzini has selected some of his favorite movie music right out of the opera repertoire. This interesting examination of the powerful use of arias and opera music in modern cinema will enlighten and entertain. These live performance lectures are lots of fun and we'd like to see you there.

Thursday, Nov. 13 at 6:00 p.m. at the Music Gallery, 5990 Ulmerton Road, Clearwater. Tickets are only ($17 for seniors ($20 general admission) Program repeats on Friday morning at 11:00 a.m. at the Museum of Fine Arts, St. Petersburg. 727-823-2040 or www.stpeteopera.org

Florida Opera Festival 2015 presented by Opera Tampa

A discounted festival 4-pack is again being offered to Pinellas Opera League Members for the upcoming 2015 season. The deadline for ordering tickets through Wayne Green has been extended through our next luncheon on January 7. Included are:

  • New Voices Concert on Sunday January 18th at 2:00 pm
  • Rigoletto on Sunday, February 15th at 2:00 pm
  • Marcello Giordani the Met’s favorite tenor Saturday, February 28th at 7:00 pm
  • Madama Butterfly on Sunday, March 15th at 2:00 pm

The festival package also includes round trip bus transportation from the Clearwater Mall (Gulf to Bay Blvd at Hwy 19) to the Straz Center for each performance. The full package cost is $195 or single performance tickets are available for $70 each. Tickets may be purchased at our luncheons or by printing and mailing the attached order form to Wayne Green (Special Events Coordinator) at the address indicated on the form.

Please advise if you need special assistance for seating on arrival at the Straz Center. Pickup 12:30 p.m. Clearwater Mall-Us 19 and Gulf-To-Bay (Lowe’s Garden Center parking lot) and 5:30 p.m. for the evening performance by Marcello Giordani.

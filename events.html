Pinellas Opera League presents DVD Opera

Because the Safety Harbor Library is not available to us on Sundays for the next few months, we will be showing our DVDs at different times and different days until June.

Saturday, May 16, 2015 we will show Mefistofele by Faust, starring Samuel Ramey with the chorus and orchestra of the San Francisco Opera from 1:00 to 5:00 p.m. Running time is 159 minutes.

Safety Harbor Public Library, 101 2nd Street North, Safety Harbor, FL 34695. The library door opens at 9:30 a.m. and the video begins at 1:00 p.m.

Free and open to the public. There is free parking behind the library. For more information call Richard Davidson at 727-242-6486

Scholarship Update

Pinellas Opera League is partnering with the Pinellas County School Board and the St. Petersburg College Foundation to bring a classical music program to an elementary school.

The Pinellas Opera League has set up a $500.00 scholarship to benefit students of the opera workshop as a community partner with the St. Petersburg College Foundation.

The Mary Jo Bell Memorial Scholarship Fund is $500.00. Help us reach our goal or better.

President Christina Alexander has won the “Peoples’ Choice Scholarship Award of Excellence” from the Eastern Educational Research Association for the three minute thesis competition sponsored by the University of Queensland in Australia.

Donations for the Arts

Gifts from generous people like you make the Pinellas Opera League an important part of the region's cultural, educational and economic growth.

As you may know, we are constantly reviewing up and coming local talent with the intent of providing scholarships. We also reach out to the community with educational programs and other activities for the performing arts. The wonderful talent that we have, right here, should be nurtured and supported. With a charitable contribution, you can receive a break on your taxes AND have the satisfaction of supporting a noble cause.

You can make your donation by mailing a check to Pinellas Opera League or using Paypal.

For Paypal press the Donate button. This opens the Paypal page. If you are not asked about payment options press CONTINUE at the lower part of the page and follow instructions.

And, remember to use your tax-deductible receipt at tax time. We thank you for your consideration!

With warmest wishes,

Christina Alexander,
President

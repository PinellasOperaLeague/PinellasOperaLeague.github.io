Membership

Membership dues for a calendar year of January 1 to December 31 are $25.00 per person and $40.00 per couple or family. Your membership allows you to meet and socialize with others interested in opera appreciation and the arts, receive our Monthly Newsletter, attend our various activities and take advantage of the discounts we offer. In addition you will be supporting our outreach programs to the communities and school systems in the Tampa Bay area. If you are interested in joining, you may use one of the methods below:

By Phone

Call Ursula Wignall, (727) 796-7260. She will answer any of your questions and send you an application.

By Mail

Download an application, print it and mail it in using the address on the application.

In Person

Come to one of our events and pick up an application.

Next luncheon Wednesday May 07, 2014.
 

Regular luncheon meetings are a fun way to socialize and meet others who enjoy opera and music.  On the first Wednesday of the month October to June the League sponsors a luncheon at the Dunedin Country Club. 1050 Palm Blvd., Dunedin, FL 34698 (727) 733-2134.  Cost is $20.  In addition to a delicious lunch, there is entertainment by local opera talent displaying their vocal abilities. 

The luncheon meetings begin at 11:00 am with a time for socializing. Business 11:30, program 11:45 and lunch 12:30.

 The League is obligated to pay for all reserved luncheons.  Reservations not cancelled at least two days prior to the luncheon and no shows will be billed.

The Pinellas Opera League is a not for profit organization dedicated to promoting the appreciation of opera and musical theater in the Tampa Bay area. The League achieves this through outreach programs in the schools and community and by sponsoring special events and discounts to local productions..

Members bring a guest and have a great time.  All are welcome.

We invite you to join us to socialize and meet other opera lovers for a fun afternoon to experience the voices of local opera talent.

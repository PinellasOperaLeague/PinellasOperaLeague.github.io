Local Opera

  

Membership dues for a calendar year of January 1 to December 31 are $25.00 per person and $40.00 per couple or family. Your membership allows you to meet and socialize with others interested in opera appreciation and the arts, receive our Monthly Newsletter, attend our various activities and take advantage of the discounts we offer. In addition you will be supporting our outreach programs to the communities and school systems in the Tampa Bay area. If you are interested in joining, you may print and use the online Membership Application in PDF (Adobe Reader)

or

You can obtain a Membership Application by attending one of our events

or

You can call Ursula Wignall, (727) 796-7260. She will answer any of your questions and send you an application.

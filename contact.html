Letter from our President

Dear members of the Pinellas Opera League

As I am nearing the end of my second term as President of Pinellas Opera League, it has been my privilege and pleasure to serve as President for the past four years. I have enjoyed meeting and working with you during this time to make a positive impact in the community in the promotion of opera and musical theater. I am honored to have earned your trust during my tenure. Regretfully, I am not able to accept the nomination for President for another term because of increased work obligations and the acceptance of a fellowship in education. I will continue to be available in an advisory capacity. Thank you for your support of opera and of Pinellas Opera League’s charitable outreach services. It is important that POL members attend the December luncheon in order to see the spectacular perfor- mances and to vote. I am looking forward to seeing all of you there.

With Warmest Regards, Christina Alexander

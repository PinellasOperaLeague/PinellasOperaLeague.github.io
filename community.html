Opera in the Community

There are many opportunities to enjoy Opera in the Tampa Bay area. We hope to see you there!

Events in the Bay Area

St. Petersburg Opera

Tickets for all SPO events can be obtained by calling 727-823-2040 or by visiting the SPO website, www.stpeteopera.org

St. Petersburg Opera Company is offering tickets including bus transportation to the Sunday matinees for their upcoming season. If you already have tickets for the Sunday matinees and would like to take advantage of the bus transportation you can order just the bus. Be advised that free parking around the Palladium is very limited this year so the bus may be a good alternative. Call Wayne Green at 727-470-9537 for forms.

Evenings and Mornings with the Maestro

Mornings with the Maestro from the St. Petersburg Opera Company are held at the Museum of Fine Arts and sponsored by Marilyn J. Ward with Maestro Mark Sforzini, Artistic and Executive Director directing. Evenings with the Maestro is sponsored by the Bailey Family: Keith, Lucy, and Wilma and are held at the Music Gallery, 5990 Ulmerton Rd., Clearwater.

Ruth Eckerd Hall

Ruth Eckerd Hall is not currently showing operas, but they do have Broadway musicals. Please check with the box office.

Tickets 727-791-7400

Tarpon Springs

New Century Opera produces, with Constantine Grame, Executive and Artistic Director, several operas during the year, in English.

For more information, visit www.tarponarts.org or call 727.942.5605.

Live HD Broadcasts

HD broadcasts of several operas for each season will begin in October. Eckerd College wll be a new venue for the broadcasts. Also at the AMC Woodlands Square in Oldsmar, Pinellas Park Regal Theaters, and at the Regal Citrus Park in Tampa. Tickets for seniors are approx $23.

Encores at AMC Woodlands Square in Oldsmar and Pinellas Park Regal Theaters are at 6:30 pm on the Wednesday following the original broadcast.

Live from the Royal Theater in London

The AMC Cobb Theatesr in Tampa Bay are occasionally showing operas live from the Royal Theater in London, the same as Live from the Met is shown locally. Check the Cobb Theater for listings as well as show times. Address 2700 US 19 N. Clearwater, FL. 33761; 727-683-0009

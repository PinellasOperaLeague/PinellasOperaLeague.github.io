St. Petersburg Opera

SPO proudly announces its TENTH season for 2015-2016. It will include productions of The Abduction from the Seraglio, La Bohème and South Pacific,. Season ticket brochures are in the mail. If you did not receive one, please call our office and we’ll be happy to send one out to you.

Abduction performances are February 5, 7, and 9. Get your tickets now to be “On the Bus” for the Sunday matinee. You can call Wayne Green for forms, or call direct to the SPO Box Office at 727-823-2040

Tickets for all SPO events can be obtained by calling 727-823-2040 or by visiting the SPO website, www.stpeteopera.org

St. Petersburg Opera Company is offering tickets including bus transportation to the Sunday matinees for their upcoming season with three more productions in 2016: Abduction from the Seraglio, La Boheme, and South Pacific. If you already have tickets for the Sunday matinees and would like to take advantage of the bus transportation you can order just the bus. Be advised that free parking around the Palladium is very limited this year so the bus may be a good alternative. Call Wayne Green at 727-470-9537 for forms.

Matinee Opera Players

The Matinee Opera Players give four to six performances each year. They do not perform an entire opera; rather, they perform arias from well-known operas and a few well-known tunes. The entire cast consists of volunteers. The next performance is at the Performing Arts Center, 4951 78th Ave., Pinellas Park.

  • Sun. Feb. 28 at 3 pm: Local talents will perform selected arias and well-known show tunes at the Pinellas Park Performing Arts Center, 4951 78th Ave., Pinellas Park, FL.

    • All performances are FREE and donations are gratefully accepted.

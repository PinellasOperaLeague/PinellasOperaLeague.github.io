Opera in the Community

There are many opportunities to enjoy Opera in the Tampa Bay area. We hope to see you there!

Events in the Bay Area

Live HD Broadcasts

HD broadcasts of eleven operas for next season will begin in October. This year, Eckerd College wll be a new venue for the broadcasts. Also at the AMC Woodlands Square in Oldsmar, Pinellas Park Regal Theaters, and at the Regal Citrus Park in Tampa. Tickets for seniors are $23.

St. Petersburg Opera

SPO proudly announces its TENTH season for 2015-2016. It will include productions of Carmen, The Abduction from the Seraglio, La Bohème, South Pacific, and Seasonal Sparkle. Season ticket brochures are in the mail. If you did not receive one, please call our office and we’ll be happy to send one out to you.

Opera at the Dalí

Thursdays at 6:00 p.m. Emerging opera artists will give 20 minute performances on the spiral staircase at the museum.

The Dalí museum is at 1 Dali Blvd (Bayshore Dr) SE and 5th Ave SE. Call 727-823-3767 for the weekly opera performers singing

Live from the Royal Theater in London

The AMC Cobb Theater at the Westfield Countryside Mall is occasionally showing operas live from the Royal Theater in London, the same as Live from the Met is shown locally. Check the Cobb Theater for listings as well as show times. Address 2700 US 19 N. Clearwater, FL. 33761; 727-683-0009

St. Petersburg Opera

Fairy Tale Opera

February’s Evenings and Mornings with Maestro Mark Sforzini and SPO singers open the book on fairy tales that have come to life in opera. Sometimes surprising, often magical, and always entertaining, these stories have endured for generations, and these charming operatic interpretations of them delight modern audiences to this day. Join us to learn more about tales that have gone from the page to the stage, where it will live happily ever after.

Evenings at 6:00 p.m. at the Music Gallery, 5990 Ulmerton Rd., Clearwater. Mornings at 11:00 at Opera Central, 2145 First Ave., S., St. Petersburg, for February only.

Tickets for all SPO events can be obtained by calling 727-823-2040 or by visiting the SPO website, www.stpeteopera.org

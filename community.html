Opera in the Community

There are many opportunities to enjoy Opera in the Tampa Bay area. We hope to see you there!

St. Petersburg Opera

Opera Idol

A new crop of singers will work with Maestro Sforzini for five weeks and then compete in a fun-filled evening of song. Only one will be crowned Opera Idol by the audience! Saturday, November 22, at 7:30 p.m. at Opera Central, 2145 First Ave. S., St. Petersburg. 727-823-2040 for tickets.

Silver Bells

St. Petersburg Opera Co. and Maestro Mark Sforzini will present an original revue, featuring SPO singers and orchestra, at Silver Bells in Sevilla, the Florida Suncoast Opera Guild's annual holiday luncheon on Friday, December 5, 2014 at 11:00 a.m. Held in the lavishly decorated St. Petersburg Yacht Club, the entertainment is a highlight of the holiday social scene.

Silver Bells is the principal fundraiser for the opera guild and the proceeds go to fund educational programs at St. Petersburg Opera and to sponsor the annual competitions for young singers. St. Petersburg Yacht Club, 11 Central Ave., St. Petersburg. 33701. Tickets $75. Advance reservations required. Info at 727-367-2975.

Seasonal Sparkle

Not one, but two singers with Metropolitan Opera credits will be joining SPO to celebrate the glory of the season. A holiday tradition, Seasonal Sparkle is a banquet of great music and spectacular performances. The magnificent St. Petersburg Opera Chorus and 40 piece professional orchestra will serve a bounty of song from the British Isles, Germany, and the U.S. including Vaughan Williams Fantasia on Christmas Carols, Mahler Songs from the Youth's Magic Horn, as well as traditional English carols, and other seasonal favorites like "Sleigh Ride" and "O Holy Night."

You will savor our international soloists, including Metropolitan Opera stars Eliza- beth Futral and Galen Bower as well as Op- eratini, SPO's four part harmony quartet featuring two Opera Idols! It's a feast for the whole family and sure to put you in a festive mood!

The Palladium Theater, 253 Fifth Ave., St. Petersburg, FL 33701 Tickets from $22 to $67, with special pricing for families. Call 727-823-2040 for details. Or purchase tickets online at www.stpeteopera.org. Special holiday refreshments from Everything Dolce will be available for purchase at the Palladium. Bus transportation from Clearwater may be available for the Sunday matinee. Call 727-823-2040 for pricing and details.

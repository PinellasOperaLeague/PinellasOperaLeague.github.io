St. Petersburg Opera

Musical Therapy

March Evenings and Mornings with the Maestro. We can't deny it any longer - some opera characters are a little crazy! Or we watch them go nuts right before our eyes. Who doesn't love a good mad-scene? Watching the heroine lose her grip is a favorite of opera-lovers the world over. Why do we identify with these whacko characters so easily? Join Maestro Sforzini for group therapy at its finest.

Evenings at 6:00 p.m. at the Music Gallery, 5990 Ulmerton Rd., Clearwater. Mornings at 11:00 at Opera Central, 2145 First Ave., S., St. Petersburg.

Tickets for all SPO events can be obtained by calling 727-823-2040 or by visiting the SPO website, www.stpeteopera.org

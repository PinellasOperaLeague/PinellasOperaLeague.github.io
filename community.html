Opera at the Dalí

Thursdays at 6:00 p.m. Emerging opera artists will give 20 minute performances on the spiral staircase at the museum.

The Dalí museum is at 1 Dali Blvd (Bayshore Dr) SE and 5th Ave SE. Call 727-823-3767 for the weekly opera performers singing

Live from the Royal Theater in London

The AMC Cobb Theater at the Westfield Countryside Mall is occasionally showing operas live from the Royal Theater in London, the same as Live from the Met is shown locally. Check the Cobb Theater for listings as well as show times on your internet. Address 2700 US 19 N. Clearwater, FL. 33761; 727-683-0009
